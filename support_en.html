1. Product Introduction

Crypto Vault is a local file encryption and decryption tool that provides secure and reliable file storage space for your important files.


When your computer needs maintenance or is temporarily borrowed, you can rest assured that your privacy content will not be accessed by others. Crypto Vault encrypts sensitive files and performs secure access, effectively preventing information leakage. Without a password, others cannot enter your encrypted space; without a key, they cannot decrypt and view your privacy content.

💡 Warning: Do not let your personal data be exposed on the internet. Keep your data secure and private.

2. Feature Descriptions

Login Access

Needs a password to enter the application, preventing files from being viewed and browsed by others. Your data can only be accessed and viewed by yourself.

File Encryption and Decryption

Files imported into the application will be encrypted and stored. When opening and exporting files, they will be decrypted to view and use them.

Key Backup and Importation

Provides the ability to back up and import keys, preventing loss loss of keys and data migration.

Data Backup and Restoration

Provides the ability to back up and restore data, preventing loss loss of data.
